iScaffold

More than a framework, less than a CMS. A CASE tool designed for developers who want rapid results.

About iScaffold

iScaffold is a CASE application built upon CodeIgniter that lets you generate your basic Models, Views and Controllers for each table in your database. iScaffold creates a complete, working CodeIgniter application that is password protected, beautifully desigened, easy to use.
